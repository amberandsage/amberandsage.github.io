Right in the Neighborhood

Riverview is one of Chattanooga's quietest, most beautiful pockets — tree-lined streets, river views, and a real sense of being away from the city while still being inside it. Amber & Sage sits at 1202 Hixson Pike, an easy walk, bike, or two-minute drive from most of the neighborhood. We're between Cherokee Boulevard and Manufacturers Road, on the way toward the North Shore.

If you've searched "day spa Riverview Chattanooga" or "massage near Riverview," this is the page that explains exactly what's at the address. We built our space with neighbors in mind — the kind of place you can stop into after a walk along the river or before brunch at one of the local spots.

What's inside

Riverview FAQ

Can I walk over from Riverview?

Yes — most of the neighborhood is a 5–15 minute walk away. The sidewalk along Hixson Pike is comfortable, and we're set back from the road in a quiet building.

Is there a "neighbor" discount or local package?

We rotate seasonal promotions (Two-for-Tuesday, etc.) and discounted memberships for regulars. Ask when you call or check the promo banner on our homepage.

Do you have showers or somewhere to change?

Yes to both, just not in the way you might be picturing. We have a shower available if you need one, and while we don't have dedicated changing rooms, your treatment room is fully private — that's where you'll change before and after your service. Robes and slippers are provided.

What if I need to bring my baby with me?

We love babies but our space is built around a quiet, unstimulating environment for everyone in treatment. For prenatal and new-mom clients, we recommend arranging childcare so you can fully unplug — if that's not possible, text us first and we'll talk through options.
