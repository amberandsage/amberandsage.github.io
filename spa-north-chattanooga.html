Your Neighborhood Spa, Right on Hixson Pike

Amber & Sage Spa sits at 1202 Hixson Pike in the heart of North Chattanooga — the same stretch that locals already know for Niedlov's Bakery, Hutton & Smith Brewing, and the climb up to Stringer's Ridge. We're a five-minute drive from Frazier Avenue, less than ten from the Tennessee Aquarium, and an easy lunchtime escape for anyone working downtown or on the Northshore.

If you've been Googling "day spa near North Shore Chattanooga" or "massage near Stringer's Ridge," you've found us. This page exists because so many of our regulars walk or bike here from the surrounding neighborhoods — and we want you to know exactly what we offer before you book.

Holistic services, all under one roof

Most spas focus on one thing. We're built around the idea that nervous-system reset, skincare, and bodywork all belong in the same conversation. You can come in for a single Eminence organic facial, or build a half-day with a massage, a sound bath, and a reiki session back-to-back. Some of what you'll find on our menu:

  • Organic facials — Eminence-partnered, with dermaplaning, enzyme peels, oxygen therapy, facial cupping, red light, and high frequency enhancements. Buccal Facial Massage is offered as a standalone 2-hour service.
  • Therapeutic massage — deep tissue, Swedish, East Asian style, hot stone, prenatal, cupping, and Graston technique.
  • Reiki & sound therapy — energy work and sound baths for nervous-system reset.
  • Yoga & meditation — guided yin and restorative classes in our studio.
  • Waxing, brows & lashes — full body waxing, lash lifts, brow lamination, and tinting.

Walkable from the North Shore

From Frazier Avenue, head north on Forrest Avenue and turn right onto Hixson Pike. You'll find us on the right just past Manufacturers Road. Free parking on-site — one less thing to think about.

Why our North Chattanooga regulars keep coming back

Our team lives here. Most of them grew up in or around Chattanooga, and a few literally walk to work. That changes the feel of a visit — you'll be greeted by name, your therapist will remember what you asked about last time, and your treatment will be built around what your body actually needs that day instead of a one-size-fits-all menu.

We're also intentionally small. There's no rush between appointments, no upsell pressure, and no commission-driven retail push. Just an unhurried space designed to let you exhale.

North Chattanooga FAQ

Is parking easy on Hixson Pike?

Yes — we have free dedicated parking right at our location at 1202 Hixson Pike. You won't need to circle the block or fight for street parking on Frazier.

Can I walk to Amber & Sage from the North Shore?

Absolutely. From Frazier Avenue, it's about a 12-minute walk up Forrest Avenue to Hixson Pike. Many of our North Shore regulars make a morning of it — coffee at Mean Mug, massage with us, lunch back on Frazier.

Do you take same-day appointments?

Often, yes — especially mid-week. The fastest way to check is to text us at (423) 537-4221 or call (423) 218-2841. We'll let you know what's open.

Are your products organic?

For skincare, yes. We're a Proudly Partnered Eminence Organic Skin Care provider — every facial uses organic, biodynamic, sustainably sourced products. Massage oils are also clean and unscented unless you request aromatherapy.
