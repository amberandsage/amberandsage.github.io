Questions

Before You Visit

  • Arrive a few minutes early to settle in. We'll chat about your goals, any sensitivities, and what you're hoping to get out of your session. From there, your therapist will personalize everything to you. Just relax — we handle the rest.

  • We operate by appointment only, which means you'll always have a quiet, personal experience. You can book online anytime, or text us for same-day availability — we'll do our best to fit you in.

  • We're proud partners with Eminence Organic Skin Care — award-winning, non-toxic, and organic products crafted from the finest natural ingredients. For massage, we use our own proprietary blends designed for maximum relaxation.

  • We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. This helps us offer those time slots to other guests who may be waiting. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.

  • Yes! We have free parking right at our location at 1202 Hixson Pike. One less thing to worry about before your appointment.
