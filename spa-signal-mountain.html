An Easy Drive Off the Mountain

From the top of Signal Mountain, head down W Road or the Boulevard — either way, you'll be at our door in about 15 minutes. We're at 1202 Hixson Pike, on the right just before you reach the Bi-Lo Stadium / Manufacturers Road area. No bridges to cross, no downtown traffic, no parking garages.

If you've been searching for "day spa near Signal Mountain TN" or "massage Walden TN," you've already noticed there isn't much on the mountain itself. We hear that from new clients every week — and it's exactly why we built a holistic, by-appointment-only spa just minutes off the mountain.

What you'll find here

We're not a chain. We're a small, locally owned spa intentionally built around the idea that real wellness blends bodywork, skincare, and nervous-system care. Most of our Signal Mountain clients are coming for one of these:

  • Eminence organic facials — biodynamic skincare with dermaplaning, enzyme peels, oxygen therapy, facial cupping, red light therapy, and high frequency enhancements. Buccal Facial Massage offered as a standalone 2-hour service.
  • Therapeutic massage — deep tissue, Swedish, East Asian style, hot stone, prenatal, cupping, and Graston for the trail runners and climbers among us.
  • Reiki & energy work — for stress relief, grief, and nervous-system reset.
  • Sound baths & yin yoga — immersive group sessions, perfect to pair with a private treatment.
  • Waxing, brows & lashes — full body waxing, lash lifts, brow lamination, and tinting.

Tip for Signal Mountain regulars

Book a half-day. Many of our mountain clients pair an Eminence facial with a 90-minute deep tissue massage — one trip down, two services, and you're back home before dinner. Mention "stacked" when you book and we'll keep the rooms side-by-side.

Why mountain residents choose us

You already know the drive-into-town tax: one errand turns into a whole afternoon. We're before all of that. You can be in the chair within 20 minutes of leaving Olde Mill or the Brow, and back home before the school pickup line forms. Free parking on-site, no valet, no garage tickets.

And we know the demographic: hikers, cyclists, climbers, folks chasing their kids up Rainbow Lake trails. We see a lot of IT bands, rotator cuffs, and tight thoracic spines. Our massage team specializes in functional bodywork, not just relaxation — though we'll absolutely do that too if you ask.
