Why a Custom Approach Instead of Packages

Most spas push you into pre-built "bachelorette packages" or "corporate retreat packages" priced per person, with rigid service menus and the same template for every group. We've chosen not to do that. Every event we host is built around the actual group — the people, the occasion, the energy, the budget — not slotted into a marketing menu.

What that means in practice: when you reach out, we'll have a real conversation about what you're imagining. A bachelorette party that wants champagne, facials, and an aura photography session is a different event than one centered around a quiet sound bath and massage. A corporate wellness day with the leadership team is a different event than one for sales reps. We build accordingly.

The Kinds of Events We Host

Bachelorette parties

Our most-frequent private event by a wide margin. Most bachelorette groups mix multiple services across the visit — facials, massages, aura photography, sometimes a sound bath — and treat the spa as the calm anchor of an otherwise busy Chattanooga weekend. We're located minutes from the North Shore restaurants and downtown, making logistics easy. Groups usually arrange champagne or sparkling beverages; we provide the rest.

Corporate wellness & team events

For HR teams and founders looking for something more meaningful than a standard team-building day. We've hosted leadership offsites, sales-team appreciation days, and wellness-focused company outings. The format scales with the group: rotating service stations, all-team sound baths, individual massage and facial slots, or some combination. We can also arrange invoicing and group billing to make procurement easy.

Birthdays & milestone celebrations

Big birthdays, 40ths and 50ths especially. Anniversary gatherings. Mother–daughter weekends. Friend reunions. These events often blend a celebratory feel with a deep need for actual rest. We design the day around both.

Bridal parties (day-of and prep visits)

For Chattanooga weddings, we host both bridal-party prep visits (the week before the wedding) and the morning-of group spa experience for brides and their attendants. We've worked with wedding coordinators across the region.

Wellness retreats & small groups

Yoga teachers, life coaches, therapists, and small wellness communities sometimes book our space as a partial-day or full-day retreat anchor — combining their own programming with our treatments and the studio space.

Anything you can imagine, really

Engagement celebrations, divorce-recovery weekends, postpartum mother circles, fundraising appreciation events, holiday gatherings, "I just need this with my best friend" days. If you can picture it, we can probably build it.

A note on couples

We don't offer traditional side-by-side couples massage in the same room. What we can do is schedule two massages simultaneously in adjoining rooms — you and your partner start and finish at the same time, then meet in the relaxation area afterward. Many of our regulars actually prefer this format. Just mention it when you reach out.

How Booking an Event Works

Step 1 — Reach out

Tell us the basics: how many people, rough date or date range, the occasion, and what you're imagining. Even if your vision is hazy, that's fine — we'll help you shape it.

Step 2 — A short conversation

We'll respond with questions and ideas, usually by text or email. For larger events, a 15-minute phone or video call is often easier than a long email thread.

Step 3 — A proposed flow

We'll send back a proposed event flow with services, timing, and pricing. You react, we adjust, repeat as needed until it feels right.

Step 4 — A deposit and the date is held

Once we agree on the plan, we hold the date with a deposit. The remainder is settled the day of (or via invoice for corporate clients).

Step 5 — Day-of execution

You arrive, your group is welcomed by name, your treatments unfold on the timing we agreed to, and the only thing your group has to decide is whether to add the aura sessions on a whim (the answer is usually yes).

Frequently Asked Questions

How many people can you accommodate for a private event?

Most groups range from 4 to 12 people. We can sometimes accommodate larger groups depending on the mix of services and scheduling. Reach out to discuss your specific group size and we'll let you know what works.

Do you offer pre-built spa party packages?

No — every private event we host is built around your group's specific vision, preferences, and budget. That means no rigid "bridal package" or "corporate package" menus. We'll talk through what you're imagining and design something that fits.

Can we have the spa to ourselves?

For larger or higher-priority events, we can buy out the full spa or a portion of it for your group's exclusive use. This is most common for bachelorette parties, milestone birthdays, and corporate retreats. Let us know when you reach out and we'll discuss options.

Can we bring alcohol or food?

Light beverages and small bites are welcome for most events — champagne for bachelorettes, lunch for corporate days, cake for birthdays. We'll talk through what makes sense and any space considerations. Heavy meals and heavily aromatic food are best handled before or after the spa portion of your day.

How far in advance should we book?

For weekend dates, 6–10 weeks ahead is ideal. Weekdays often have more flexibility, sometimes bookable within 2–3 weeks. Bachelorette weekends and bridal weekends fill earliest, especially spring and fall.

Do you handle corporate billing and invoicing?

Yes. For corporate clients we can issue an invoice with W-9 documentation, accept ACH or check payment, and handle whatever your AP team needs. Mention this when you reach out and we'll set it up.

Can you bring services to us off-site?

For most events we host you here in our space, which is built for the unhurried experience we're known for. For specific corporate or off-site situations, reach out and we'll discuss whether something custom is possible.
