How It Works

1. Pick an amount

Choose from standard preset values or enter a custom amount that fits your budget and your intent. A small gift card covers a single service; larger amounts allow for a longer visit or multi-service experience.

2. Add a personal note (optional)

Include a message that the recipient will see when the card arrives. A few sentences from you means more than the dollar amount.

3. Send instantly

The gift card is delivered via email within seconds — either directly to the recipient or to you to forward when you're ready. No shipping. No waiting. Great for last-minute gifts.

4. They redeem when ready

The recipient calls, texts, or books online at their convenience. Five years to redeem — they can use it next week or three years from now. Balance can be used in full or partially.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do your gift cards expire?

Our gift cards are valid for 5 years from the date of purchase — plenty of time for your recipient to use them when they're ready. There are no maintenance fees that erode the balance over time.

How quickly does the gift card arrive?

Instantly. Digital gift cards are emailed to the recipient (or to you for forwarding) within seconds of purchase. Perfect for last-minute gifts.

Can the gift card be used for any service?

Yes. Gift cards are redeemable for any service we offer — facials, massage, reiki, sound baths, aura photography, yoga, waxing — plus retail products. The recipient chooses what they want.

Can I choose a specific dollar amount?

Yes. Gift cards are available in standard preset denominations and as custom amounts. Pick what fits your budget and the experience you want them to have.

Can I add a personal message?

Yes — there's a message field at checkout. Your note is included in the email that goes to the recipient.

Can a gift card cover part of a larger service?

Absolutely. Your recipient can use the full balance toward a single service or apply it as a partial payment toward a longer visit and pay the difference at checkout.

Do you offer physical gift cards?

Yes — two ways to get one. We have a 24/7 self-serve gift card box outside our front entrance, so you can grab a physical card any time of day or night, even when we're closed. For a more curated experience — or if you'd like one wrapped or packaged with a retail product — book a quick retail appointment and we'll set you up in person.
