A fresh face...

Amber & Sage Spa and Boutique is taking over the spa formally known as Natural Body. We are going to have a fresh look and new owners but offer the same amazing services from some of the same great therapists. We will continue to be provide premium Eminence Organic Skin Care in our services.

Online booking will be coming soon as we transition to a "by-appointment-only" model Monday through Friday, but don't worry, we will have plenty of time and services avilable and the booking process will be easier than ever.

Our exclusive boutique will be publicly open on weekends and will be an evolving carefully curated collection of "clean" and effective beauty, skincare, wellness, and lifestyle products.

We really appreciate your patience during our transition and genuinely hope we can look forward to your continued support throughout this process.

Be sure to fill out our contact form to stay up-to-date with booking availablity updates, opening dates, promotions, and more!

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ If you're excited for us to open and can't wait to book a service, please leave us a 5 star review on Google to let us know! ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
